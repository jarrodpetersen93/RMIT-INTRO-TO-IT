My Project Idea

My project idea is for a full cycle insurance management platform. Where my project distinguishes itself from its competitors is in the breadth of the insurer markets it will approach & the post-purchase service that is not provided by other aggregator websites. Current insurance comparison websites simply give you a price, facilitate the purchase and then leave the buyer to deal with the insurer directly. Switch Insurance, my project, will provide facilities to lodge and maintain claims, make mid-term changes & remarket policies at renewal.

My motivation to develop this project is based on my experience and research into the opportunity to develop a service not currently available in the Australian Insurance market. In the 2019 calendar year, $19.6 Billion worth of premium was written in the Australian domestic insurance market. Based on my proposed pricing model 1% market share would represent $46 million dollars in turnover. While every company is founded on the idea of profit, I intend to offer a more transparent & fair process. Current insurance comparison websites only canvas a very small percentage of the available insurers, one particular comparison website is also part of the same holding company that owns an insurer that regularly finds itself at the top of their ‘comparison’.

The main feature of the service will be product & price comparison. My goal is to have at least 5-8 insurance partners available to canvas a significant portion of the available market. The project will have a responsive webpage optimised for small screens as well as an android & iOS application. Switch intends to provide services for the customer throughout the life-cycle of their policy. The claim lodgment function will be a significant value-add of the service, providing a single of contact between the insurer & client while also providing a service that advocates & provides advice for the client. The landing page would simplify the process of getting a quote & creating a user login. To be feasible, development would need to include three systems for admins, insurers & end users. Each of them with different user interfaces & functions.
